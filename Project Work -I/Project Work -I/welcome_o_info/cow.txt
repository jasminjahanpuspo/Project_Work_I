1.Cows are members of the sub-family ‘Bovinae’ of the family ‘Bovidae’.
2.Today, cows are domesticated ungulates (hoofed animals with two toes 
on each hoof) that we see very often chewing the grass in farmers fields as we walk or drive through the countryside.
3.The mature female of the species is called a ‘cow’.
4.The mature male of the species is called a ‘bull’.
5.A cow spends up to 6 hours a day eating.
