The tiger is the largest cat species, most recognizable for its pattern of dark vertical 
stripes on reddish-orange fur with a lighter underside. The species is classified in the genus
 Panthera with the lion, jaguar, leopard, and snow leopard. 
Family: Felidae
Lifespan: 16 – 18 years (In captivity)
Order: Carnivora
Class: Mammalia
Mass: Male: 90 – 310 kg (Adult), Female: 65 – 170 kg (Adult)
